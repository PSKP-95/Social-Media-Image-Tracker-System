PERCEPTUAL HASHING



There can also be an errorneous situation if the user changes the image in between ,which may lead to change in information. This may lead to chaos and confusion.

Solution to the above problem is using Perceptual Hashing which can help us in checking the similarity between the incoming and outcoming image

How is it done?

If the images are identical, the information will be copied to new image and chain will be continued.
